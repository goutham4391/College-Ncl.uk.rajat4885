NUMed Malaysia

Our purpose-built campus, world-class staff, and students are the heart of the Newcastle community in Malaysia. We deliver high-quality medical and biomedical education.

  • Newcastle University
  • Newcastle University Medicine Malaysia

Our graduates are globally aware but regionally based. We prepare them for careers that will underpin the health and welfare of society.

Undergraduate

Our medical and biomedical sciences training is comparable to Newcastle University's. It's modified to be relevant to Malaysia.

How to apply

NUMed Malaysia has one intake per year for its undergraduate programme and two intakes for it foundation programme.

International

If you're travelling to Johor, Malaysia, from abroad, we'll welcome you at Kuala Lumpur International Airport. We make sure your journey to campus is safe.

Accommodation

NUMed Malaysia's campus is located in Iskandar Puteri (formerly known as Nusajaya), near the city of Johor Bahru.

We aspire to provide globally acclaimed medical education programmes, responsive to the evolving needs of the healthcare sector, as well as to retain students of the highest academic excellence.

Prof. Chris Baldwin, Provost & CEO of NUMed

Support

Our support teams are on hand and cover a wide range of areas, from health and wellbeing to IT.
