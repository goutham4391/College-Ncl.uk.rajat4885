Explore the exciting world of Medical Sciences by joining us for a series of interactive lectures delivered by expert clinicians and academics.

Explore the exciting world of Medical Science

Our Mini-Medical School runs every autumn and is open to anyone aged 15 and over.

The programme offers the public the chance to find out about exciting topics. This includes current research developments at the University.
 
Previous programmes have covered a range of exciting topics, including:

  • anaesthetics
  • dermatology
  • emergency medicine
  • medical scanning technology
  • psychology
  • pharmacy
  • sport and exercise science

Times and dates

Evening lectures will run on Thursdays 6-7pm:

  • 03, 10, 17 and 24 November
  • 01 December 2022

The Mini Medical School will be delivered online via Zoom.
