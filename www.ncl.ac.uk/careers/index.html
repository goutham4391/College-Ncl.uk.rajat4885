Careers Service

Access us via MyCareer

Log in to book a careers appointment, sign up for events, and search for graduate jobs, work experience and part-time jobs from employers seeking Newcastle students and graduates.
Get feedback on your CV

The Careers Service can help you write a winning CV. Find out how to get feedback from our CV advisers and try out our new online CV checker, CareerSet! 
Get Ready to Graduate Pathway

If you’re not sure about what you want to do after your final year, are looking for opportunities, or are thinking about the move into the world of work, the Get Ready to Graduate Pathway has resources and activities that cover all stages of your journey.
Find jobs

Whether you’re seeking a graduate or part-time job, we can help you identify key sources of vacancies.
Start or grow a business

If you’re looking to freelance or start your own business, START UP has all you need to get going. 
Explore Occupations

Find out about different careers, including how to gain experience and find jobs in a wide range of sectors. 
Gain work experience

Find invaluable work experience in the form of paid internships (on or off campus), placement years, work shadowing and insight days.
Practise your interview

Practise your interview skills whenever you want, using our video interview platforms, Shortlist.Me and Graduates First.  
Read our blog

Visit the Experience Newcastle blog for advice and inspiration to help you explore your options and get ready for the world of work.
